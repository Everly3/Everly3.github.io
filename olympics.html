WELCOME TO THE SWEET & SPICY WEB BLOG 2024 OLYMPICS SPECIAL!

Posted Friday, August 2, 2024

If you do not want to know the results and stats of some of the olympic events, I would recommend you stop reading now. Welcome to the Sweet & Spicy super special 2024 olympic post. This summer olympics is going to be super exciting, and I am here to keep updated on some of the most popular events. Starting with women's gymnastics. The team final starts with eight teams. For each each of the four events, vault, floor, uneven bars, and beam, three people from each team compete in each event. The combined scores from the three people from each team for all the events make the country's final score. The USA starts out strong on the team final and wins gold, with 171.296 points. Trailing not to far behind, team Italy won silver, with 165.494 points. And team Brazil won bronze, with 164.497 points.
