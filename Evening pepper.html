WELCOME TO THE SWEET & SPICY WEB BLOG EVENING PEPPER PUTTANESCA RECIPE!

Posted Tuesday, July 32, 2024

This recipe is an step by step guide to create a delicious new version of Puttanesca pasta. Start with plain tomato sauce. No spices, no salt. Then pour it into a pot and spice it with spices of your choosing. If you aren't sure what spices to use, some suggestions are garlic powder, onion salt, dried oregano, dried basil, and salt. If possible, i'd recommend adding fresh thyme, oregano, and basil. Once all the spices are added, add whole peeled tommato and cut up with a knife. start cooking the sauce. Stir the sauce routinely. This part is the puttanesca element. Take out a pan and put oil in it. Put olives, garlic, and sliced peppers in that pan. Spice it with red pepper flakes and some oregano. Start it cooking. While all that is happening, cook the pasta noodles. Once the pasta noodles have finished cooking, serve everything. This recipe is a new version of puttanesca becaused of the sliced peppers added.
